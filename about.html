My Story

Born and raised in Southern Denmark with a passion for drums and different sounds, I took to rap and wrote my first lyrics in the very young age of 8. After a few years performing, I needed a shift in the music and after focusing on improving my sound, I created a new identity as Crimson Marks. I gather inspiration from the hip hop and RnB genres and base my lyrics on real events to maintain the important connection between me as an artist and my listeners.
